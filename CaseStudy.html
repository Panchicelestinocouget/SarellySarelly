Most mascaras weren't made
for Latin lashes.
Cow Lashes was.

Every beauty brand knows Latin lashes are the hardest to work with. Avon even created the Extravagant Mascara, designed for up to 15 layers, because standard formulas simply don't hold on straight, thin Latin lashes.

Sarelly didn't adapt a generic formula. They built one from scratch for this specific problem. If it works on Latin lashes, it works on everyone. That is the hook, the content strategy, and the conversion argument in one sentence.

Profiles

60%
Latinas/os with straight lashes · nano/micro 5K-50K
The core profile. This profile is already validated in Mexico, and it should work exactly the same way here. When she applies the mascara and her lashes transform on camera, the video is the proof. No script needed.
20%
Working women, not beauty-first · nano/micro 15K-80K
The mom with no time. The student. Their content is not makeup, but the mascara enters their real routine naturally. This profile generates the highest save rate because it doesn't look like an ad. It answers the question "does it actually last" with real life, not studio lighting.
20%
Natural beauty creators · nano/micro 10K-60K
People who already share more homemade recipes, natural beauty hacks, and traditional remedies their grandmother taught them. Cow Lashes is formulated with exactly those traditional ingredients. For this audience, the product isn't just a new mascara; it's the validation of what they already believe. Highest trust transfer of any profile.

Filter: US-based audience, engagement above 4%. One question before every seed: would she buy this if we didn't gift it? If no, skip.

Question 2 | Content & Creator Judgment
Content & Creator Judgment
Medicube
What they are doing particularly well

Medicube never tries to sell the expensive product first. Skincare sets at $20 to 30 build the volume, the algorithm, and the trust. Once someone bought a cream and it worked, the $300 device becomes the natural next step. Traffic builder plus margin builder. Their Live-exclusive bundle at $185 sold 19K units. Not on Amazon. Not on their site. Only during the stream.

Why it works

Medicube broke the assumption that TikTok Shop only works for impulse purchases under $20. Creators educate instead of sell. They show real skin, real routines, real transformations over time. By the time someone considers the $300 device, they have watched 10 videos from people they trust using it. The trust does the selling. And the Live-exclusive bundle creates urgency that does not exist anywhere else. If you leave the stream, the price disappears.

What I would apply to Sarelly

Cow Lashes at $24 is the traffic builder. The same community that bought the mascara is who considers a $300 bag or $150 sunglasses later. Without the mascara building the relationship, the accessories have no runway. For Lives: a Sarelly bundle that only exists during the stream gives the creator a real reason to build urgency and the viewer a real reason to buy now.

And the cultural parallel worth naming: Medicube does not hide it is Korean. Sarelly does not hide it is Mexican. Two brands from non-dominant cultures that turned their identity into their biggest competitive advantage. Both used TikTok to take that identity to the world.

Question 3 | TikTok Shop
Views up, conversion down.
Four checks in order.

The problem is somewhere between the content and the checkout.

to checkData I reviewHypothesis I testAction I prioritize
Channel Link click rate (Link CTR) vs. views in the Seller Center affiliate dashboard. High views but low clicks means the content generates interest but the purchase path is broken. High clicks but low purchases means the listing is killing the sale. Pin the link in the first comment. Add a verbal CTA in the last 3 seconds of the video. Verify the product is live and correctly mapped in Seller Center.
Expectation Comment sentiment and question patterns across the top 5 performing videos. The one-star pattern (dry, clumps, doesn't elongate) is due to users applying a specialized Latin lash formula like a generic mascara. The content is failing to set the correct expectation before they buy. Add a mandatory line to every creator brief: "designed for straight and thin Latin lashes, here is how to apply it correctly." This single adjustment reduces bad reviews and improves CVR simultaneously.
Listing Add-to-cart rate vs. purchase rate in TikTok Shop analytics. Review distribution: 442 one-star reviews visible above the fold. Users arrive with high intent, see 442 one-star reviews right at the top, and leave immediately. The social proof is working against the sale, not for it. Generate 5-star reviews urgently with a seed wave focused strictly on correct application technique. Add a pinned seller response to the most visible negative reviews explaining the specialized nature of the formula.
Offer Friction Checkout drop-off rate in TikTok Shop vs. final price including shipping and taxes. The video drives massive impulse intent, but unexpected shipping fees or long delivery estimates at the final step cause immediate cart abandonment. Launch an immediate "Free Shipping" coupon for first-time buyers or activate a friction-free logistical bundle directly on the platform.
