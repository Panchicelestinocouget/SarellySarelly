Note on ROAS targets: Sarelly MX operates at 10-15x ROAS by investing only 15% of revenue in paid marketing, relying on organic content and co-creation to drive the rest. In US launch phase (months 1-3), 2x is the floor to validate the channel. The goal is to build enough organic volume that paid is amplification, not dependency.
